ü•ó Foods That Boost Immunity

Incorporating citrus fruits, garlic, ginger, spinach, and almonds into your daily diet can significantly strengthen and support your immune system. Citrus fruits like oranges, lemons, and grapefruits are rich in vitamin C, a powerful antioxidant known to boost the production of white blood cells, which are vital for fighting infections. Garlic contains sulfur compounds that enhance the immune response and help the body ward off colds and other illnesses. Ginger, with its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, is effective in reducing inflammation and promoting overall health. Spinach is loaded with essential nutrients such as vitamins A, C, and E, magnesium, and iron, all of which contribute to immune function. Almonds, on the other hand, are a great source of vitamin E, a fat-soluble antioxidant that helps protect cells from damage and supports immune health. Including a variety of these nutrient-dense foods in your meals can provide long-term benefits and help your body stay strong and resilient.

Use meditation or mindfulness apps that guide you through relaxing sessions designed to reduce mental clutter.
